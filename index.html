About us

Welcome to Moona Creations, where artistry and passion come together to create stunning sculptures and unique decorative pieces. Founded with the vision of celebrating the beauty of craftsmanship, our brand is dedicated to bringing timeless art into contemporary spaces.

At Moona Creations, we believe that every piece tells a story. Our sculptures are meticulously handcrafted using high-quality materials, inspired by nature, mythology, and the rich tapestry of human experience. From elegant figurines to intricate statues, each creation is a reflection of our commitment to artistry and attention to detail.

We are driven by a passion for creativity and a desire to inspire. Our collection is designed to resonate with art lovers and collectors alike, offering a diverse range of styles that can elevate any environment. Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your home, a thoughtful gift, or a unique addition to your collection, Moona Creations has something for everyone.

Join us on this artistic journey as we explore the world of sculpture and craftsmanship. Discover how our creations can transform your space and bring a touch of elegance and personality to your life.

What materials are your statues made of?

At Moona Creations, our statues are meticulously handcrafted from high-quality polyresin.
We choose polyresin because it offers the ideal balance between artistic expression and durability, giving each statue a vibrant, polished appearance while maintaining its strength and resistance to the elements. Every statue is carefully sculpted and painted by hand, ensuring that each piece is unique and full of character.

How do I care for my statue?

For Indoor Display:

Dust Regularly: Gently dust your statue with a soft, dry cloth to keep it free from dust and debris.
 
Light Cleaning: If your statue needs more than dusting, you can wipe it with a damp cloth using mild soap and water. 

For Outdoor Display:

Protect from Extreme Weather:
 We recommend bringing them indoors during extreme weather conditions.


Sealing for Longevity:
You may apply a clear protective sealant designed for outdoor statues to enhance durability, especially if the statue is constantly exposed to outdoor elements.
